In the 60s Broken (brokin) English in Nigeria was the best option for communication with the white men, in the 80s Broken (brokin) English was for the low class Nigerians, presently broken (brokin) English is the generally accepted language for Nigerian, flexible to write, easy to speak and the most amazing way to communicate with in all strata of personality.

Soon it will be adopted as our official language because it is used by all and in every sector, above 75% of Nigerians make do of the language that is why we tag the dictionary the Nigerian language.

HISTORY

The Nigerian Language is a broken (brokin) English dictionary from the street of Nigeria and it is routed in warri south-south Nigeria. In warri we don’t speak pigeon English as many outside delta region will call it, we speak the waffi language generally called broken English which is the right name for our so called pigeon English, we called and describe what many call pigeon English as the waffi flow You called it pigeon English we call it broken (brokin) English. Broken (brokin) English started with the Niger delta traders at the coastline which are pre dominantly the itsekiri at the arrival of the English missionary’s and merchant traders.

Broken (brokin) English is now liken to warri which is now called the waffy laguage, there are two warri in terms of geopolitical divison, the warri north and the warri south, the warri north, mostly called the warri township is we're the pigeon English (waffy language) is rooted.

Every part of the Niger delta speaks broken (brokin) English effectively: PH, Benin, Sapele, bayesa, calaba, Koko and Warri. The finest among all when it comes to broken (brokin) English is warri.

Presently there are three warri in terms of location which are old warri, new warri and big warri (ode itsekiri's)

Old warri is the first area settlements of warri where every tribe has their community and special item to denote the area

New warri is the then Bush now spring up and occupied by people in warri town called old warri.

Big warri is a tribe classification of warri by the itsekiri's which is the small warri and the big warri, to the itsekiri's the small warri is the land warri which is the warri township while the big warri is riverine which is their monarch and traditional headquarte.

warri is a land of rhetoric. Leading in wordcraft in the world because new impressive words are manufactured every day.

In warri or the waffy language rhetorically question/statement is allowed used as a medium of disagreement or not accepting what you are saying for example: come (statement) come fire (reply) , go na (statement) go where or go way tin (reply)
